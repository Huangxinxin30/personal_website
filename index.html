1. Why China, Why Now?

With over $5.5B in revenue and 500M+ Mini Game users, China is one of the fastest-growing and most lucrative markets for mobile games.

Platforms like WeChat Mini Games and Douyin (TikTok CN) now offer:

  • Easier entry points (less licensing constraints)
  • High ad monetization (top titles can reach $1M+ daily revenue)
  • Instant play access, no install required
  • Viral reach via social + video-driven discovery

3. What Makes a Game Suitable?

We're seeing the most traction in:

  • Hyper casual or hybrid casual genres
  • Easy-to-understand mechanics with short loops
  • Strong ad engagement (7+ rewarded videos/DAU)
  • Lightweight IAP layer (if licensing is needed)
Quick self-check:

Does your game have…

  • D1 Retention > 35%?
  • 30 mins avg. playtime?
  • IAP Revenue > 20%?
  • Day-14 ROI ≥ 100%:
  • Rewarded Video Impressions > 7/day/user (or 15+ over 2 days)
  • Tier-1 CPI (e.g. FB open targeting) < $0.80 USD

If so, it's worth evaluating for China. If 2+ metrics are met, the product usually has very strong China market potential.
